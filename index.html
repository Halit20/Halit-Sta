About

Thebuilderbehindthebrand.

I'm Halit — a Kosovo-based engineer who builds the full digital side of a business. Software, AI workflows, branding, media, and the infrastructure underneath: I'm comfortable across all of it.

I've been building for the internet since the early Albanian YouTube days — as one of the first Albanian-community YouTubers with MegaSHQIP in the gaming era — first content and audiences, then the systems behind them.

I didn't come up through a single lane. I've worked in institutional IT and server environments, built web platforms and SaaS interfaces, produced video and drone content, and set up the hosting that keeps it all running.

That range is the point. I understand both the technical system and the brand on top of it — so the things I build look right, work properly, and pull in the same direction.

I don't just hand over a website. I build the digital foundation a business runs on — and I stay close enough to the whole picture to make sure it actually launches.

The story

Dyshja n'Natyrë is a shared creative project by Halit Sta and Aldin Cakiqi — two close friends behind one camera. After the gaming era, they started creating cinematic Albanian outdoor content together: nature, challenges, exploration, adventure, sea, mountains, camping, hiking, and the stories in between. They are among the first Albanian creators focused on this specific kind of cinematic outdoor and adventure content.

Two friends, one camera, and the Albanian outdoors — filmed like it deserves to be seen.
